Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our tutoring platform and services.

How do I get matched with a tutor?

After signing up, you'll fill out a detailed profile about your learning goals, preferred schedule, and learning style. Our matching algorithm then suggests the best tutors for you. You can also browse tutor profiles, read reviews, and trial a session before committing.

Can I change tutors if I'm not satisfied?

Absolutely! If you're not satisfied with your tutor, you can request a change at any time. We'll work with you to find a better match. Your satisfaction is our priority, and switching tutors is completely free.

What's your refund policy?

We offer a 7-day free trial with no commitment. If you're not happy within the first month, we'll refund your payment in full. After that, you can cancel your subscription anytime, and your access will remain until the end of your billing cycle.

How do I schedule sessions?

Scheduling is easy through our platform. You can view your tutor's available time slots, book sessions that work for you, and reschedule if needed (up to 24 hours before the session). You'll receive email reminders 24 hours and 1 hour before your session.

What time zones do tutors operate in?

Our tutors primarily operate in Malawi Standard Time (MST), which allows for convenient scheduling for Malawian students. However, we also have tutors available in other time zones for international students. The platform will show available tutors based on your location.

Are sessions recorded?

Yes! All live sessions are recorded and available for you to review anytime. This is perfect for reviewing concepts you missed or for studying before exams. Recordings are available in your dashboard for up to 12 months.

Can I contact my tutor between sessions?

Yes! You can send messages to your tutor through our platform anytime. Most tutors respond within a few hours during business days. For urgent questions, you can also email our support team and we'll help connect you with your tutor.
